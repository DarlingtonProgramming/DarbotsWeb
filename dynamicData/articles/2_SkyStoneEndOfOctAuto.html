SkyStone End-Of-October Autonomous Video Released

Darbots released team 4100 SkyStone Season End-Of-October Autonomous Video on Youtube

This is our Blue Alliance Loading Site's Autonomous Programs for FTC Skystone 2019-2020 Season.

As you can see our program has improved a lot over a short period.

The first Autonomous was shot on Oct. 15 before our first meet. And the second Autonomous was shot on Oct. 24.

We were able to keep our robot squared during autonomous by implementing Gyro-Guided Drives on top of Encoder-Controlled Drives.

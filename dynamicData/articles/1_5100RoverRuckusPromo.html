Team 5100 Rover Ruckus Promo Video Released

Darbots revealed team 5100 Rover Ruckus promotion video on Youtube

Team 5100 is Darbots' Rookie Team, in 2019-2020 First Tech Challenge Rover Ruckus season, this team developed a completely different robot from the team 4100 - making a huge step up from previous years.

This team also initiates Darbots FTC Lib, an open-source FTC library that wraps up seasonal navigation / tensorflow codes as well as autonomous driving functionalities.

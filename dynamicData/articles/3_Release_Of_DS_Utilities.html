Darbots released Driver Station Utilities

Darbots released its utilities for upgrading Driver Station APP versions without connecting to a Wifi Network.

A lot of times, FTC teams face huge problems upgrading their Driver Station APP versions because managing a Google account for Google Play is time-consuming and torturing

Especially in a school environment which the wifis have filters on - installing CA certs, and logging to the WIFI via 802.1X on DS phones are pains that teams must overcome.

Thus today we released our utilities for upgrading DS APPs via a USB connection with a Mac / Windows Computer.

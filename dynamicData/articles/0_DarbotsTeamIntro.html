Darbots Introduction

This article describes what team Darbots is, and what mission we are pursuing on our journey of Robotics!

Main Theory

Determination, Passion, Encouragement, Gracious Professionalism

Darbots is a team that participates in the robotics competition, First Tech Challenge

Darbots is a diverse group of students, grades 6-12, with a passion to develop their talents and abilities in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. But our mission does not stop here. Darbots is constantly involved in making an impact in the STEM community.

Team Structure

The team is divded into 4 different subteams, each having their different responsibilities.

Programming

The programming subteam programs for every robot prototype and maintains the Darbots-FTC-Lib, and the Darbots Website.

The programming subteam also records programming video tutorial series

Building

The building subteam builds prototypes as well as finalized versions of our competition robots as well as our outreach project robots / IoT devices

Outreach / Business Team

The outreach subteam connects with other teams and local communities to help build a better STEM community for the world.

It manages our social media accounts and keeps track all of our outreach projects.

Research & Development Team (R&D)

The R&D subteam reflects on our competition / experiment results and looks for inspirations to improve our robot throughout the season.

It designs different robot iterations, verifies those designs with STEM skills we learned in school, and hand final designs to the building team.

Darbots is a team that participates in the robotics competition, First Tech Challenge

Darbots is a diverse group of students, grades 6-12, with a passion to develop their talents and abilities in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. But our mission does not stop here. Darbots is constantly involved in making an impact in the STEM community.
